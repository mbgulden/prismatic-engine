## Practical Guidance for a Small Harvester in Meridian

1. **First call:** Recycle Boise Inc. — **(208) 871-9432** — Ask about small-harvester pricing, acceptance of third-party material, and whether you need an account.
2. **Backup:** Visit PC Recyclers of Idaho at 1260 E. Watertower (Meridian) in person since their website is down.
3. **For larger volumes:** E Cyclers of Idaho (Idaho Falls) or METech Recycling (SLC) — both offer pickup/shipping options.
4. **NAID AAA note:** None of the Treasure Valley facilities explicitly advertise NAID AAA certification. If NAID AAA is a hard requirement, you may need to ship to a national provider or expand search radius significantly.
