accumulate decommissioned servers, switches, and networking gear that takes up 
valuable office space and creates a security liability. 

We offer a **free "Sentinel E-Waste Bin"** — we drop off a locked collection 
console, you fill it with retired gear, and we pick it up on a regular schedule. 
No cost to you, no paperwork, no compliance headaches. You get your storage 
back, and we responsibly recycle or refurbish the hardware.

We're a division of Growth Web Development, based right here in Meridian. 
When can we drop off a bin for your office?"

## Walk-in Protocol

1. Show up between 10am-11am or 2pm-3pm (avoid lunch)
2. Bring: box of donuts or pastries + business card
3. Ask front desk: "Is the IT manager available for 2 minutes?"
4. Pitch: 30-second version above
5. If they say no: leave card + 1-paragraph flyer
6. If they say yes: offer to drop the bin the same week
7. Follow up via email within 48 hours regardless
